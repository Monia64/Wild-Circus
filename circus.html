About Us

Created of the year 2000 in San francisco, the Wild Circus had celebrates its nineteen years of existence. We currently have 10 productions worldwide: Las vegas, Paris, Tokyo, Dubaï, Berlin, Sydney, Moscou, Rio, Santiago. 120 people work at the Wild Circus for you offer shows that are always more extraordinary than the others. The team is creative and modern for you to have stars full of eyes.
